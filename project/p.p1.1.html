P.P1.1 Project Ideas

Crystalis

Crystalis is an online store that sells healing crystals, pendulums, incense and other items useful for meditation and focus.

Walkie

Walkie is an online shop that specializes in customized pet products such as collars, leashes, harnesses and bowls. Products can be customized by engraving or choosing a pattern for the fabric.

Guilty Pleasures

Guilty Pleasures is an online shop that sells international snacks not eaily found in the United States. Customers can browse sweet or savory snacks and categorize them by country or region. The shop also offers a subscription box service for customers who want to try a variety of snacks from around the world
